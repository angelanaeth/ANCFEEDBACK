## ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK: 3-TIER METHODOLOGY

### TIER 1: READINESS ASSESSMENT
**Purpose**: Quick snapshot of current athlete state
**Components**:
- Training Stress Balance (TSB) interpretation
- Fatigue vs. Fitness ratio (ATL/CTL)
- Recent workout completion rate
- Sleep and recovery markers
- HRV trends (if available)

**Output**: Simple readiness score (1-10) with brief explanation

### TIER 2: SUBSYSTEM BREAKDOWN
**Purpose**: Deep dive into four critical physiological systems

#### 2.1 AEROBIC SYSTEM
- CTL trends by discipline (swim/bike/run)
- Zone 2 consistency and duration
- Aerobic decoupling metrics
- Mitochondrial density indicators

#### 2.2 THRESHOLD SYSTEM
- FTP/Threshold power trends
- Lactate threshold maintenance
- Time at threshold (Zone 4)
- Power/pace at threshold

#### 2.3 MECHANICAL SYSTEM
- Durability markers (run decoupling)
- Bike economy (kJ per km)
- Run economy (power per pace)
- Neuromuscular fatigue

#### 2.4 METABOLIC SYSTEM
- Fueling consistency
- Energy availability
- Recovery nutrition timing
- Hydration patterns

### TIER 3: FORWARD PROJECTIONS
**Purpose**: Predictive modeling and coaching recommendations

#### 3.1 CTL/ATL/TSB PROJECTIONS
- 7-day forward projection
- 14-day forward projection
- 30-day projection to race day (if applicable)
- Risk zones identified

#### 3.2 BLOCK RECOMMENDATIONS
Based on current stress state and training age:
- **Base/Durability**: TSB > 0, ATL/CTL < 1.0
- **Build/Threshold**: TSB -5 to 5, ATL/CTL 1.0-1.1
- **VO2 Max**: TSB -10 to 0, ATL/CTL 1.1-1.2
- **Specificity**: TSB -5 to 5, race < 4 weeks
- **Taper**: TSB rising, race < 2 weeks

#### 3.3 TSS RECOMMENDATIONS
Use BLOCK_CONFIGS logic:
- Calculate coming Sunday CTL/ATL/TSB
- Calculate mid-week Wednesday projections
- Apply echo estimate: (7 * CTL_sunday) / 0.965
- Factor in subjective metrics
- Output TSS range: echo * low_change to echo * high_change

## DATA INTERPRETATION RULES

### CTL (Chronic Training Load) - FITNESS
- < 50: Beginner/detrained
- 50-80: Recreational athlete
- 80-120: Competitive age-grouper
- 120-150: Elite age-grouper
- 150+: Professional level

### ATL (Acute Training Load) - FATIGUE
- ATL/CTL < 0.95: Under-training risk
- ATL/CTL 0.95-1.05: Optimal maintenance
- ATL/CTL 1.05-1.15: Productive overreach
- ATL/CTL 1.15-1.25: Functional overreach
- ATL/CTL > 1.25: Non-functional overreach risk

### TSB (Training Stress Balance) - FORM
- TSB > 10: Well-rested, may lose fitness
- TSB 0-10: Fresh, race-ready
- TSB -5 to 0: Optimal training state
- TSB -10 to -5: Building fitness, moderate fatigue
- TSB -20 to -10: Heavy training, high fatigue
- TSB < -20: Overreached, recovery needed

### HRV (Heart Rate Variability)
- Baseline + 10%: Well-recovered
- Baseline ± 5%: Normal recovery
- Baseline - 10%: Accumulating fatigue
- Baseline - 20%: Significant stress, reduce load

## STRESS LOGIC EQUATIONS

### Echo Estimate (Weekly TSS Recommendation)
```
echo_estimate = (7 * CTL_coming_sunday) / 0.965
```

### CTL Calculation (Exponential Weighted Moving Average)
```
CTL_today = CTL_yesterday + (TSS_today - CTL_yesterday) / CTL_TAU
where CTL_TAU = 42 days
```

### ATL Calculation
```
ATL_today = ATL_yesterday + (TSS_today - ATL_yesterday) / ATL_TAU
where ATL_TAU = 7 days
```

### TSB Calculation
```
TSB = CTL - ATL
```

### Future Projection
```
CTL_future = CTL_today + (planned_TSS - CTL_today) / CTL_TAU * days
ATL_future = ATL_today + (planned_TSS - ATL_today) / ATL_TAU * days
TSB_future = CTL_future - ATL_future
```

## BLOCK-SPECIFIC RAMP RULES

### BASE/DURABILITY BLOCK
- **Goal**: Build aerobic base, increase CTL gradually
- **TSS Increase**: 5-7% per week maximum
- **ATL/CTL Target**: 0.95-1.03
- **TSB Target**: -5 to +5
- **Key Sessions**: Long Z2 rides, easy runs, aerobic swims
- **Duration**: 4-8 weeks

### BUILD/THRESHOLD BLOCK
- **Goal**: Raise FTP/threshold power and pace
- **TSS Increase**: 3-5% per week
- **ATL/CTL Target**: 1.03-1.08
- **TSB Target**: -10 to 0
- **Key Sessions**: Threshold intervals, tempo work
- **Duration**: 3-6 weeks

### VO2 MAX BLOCK
- **Goal**: Improve maximal aerobic capacity
- **TSS Increase**: Maintain or slight decrease
- **ATL/CTL Target**: 1.08-1.15
- **TSB Target**: -15 to -5
- **Key Sessions**: 3-5min VO2 intervals
- **Duration**: 2-4 weeks

### SPECIFICITY BLOCK
- **Goal**: Race-specific fitness
- **TSS Increase**: Maintain CTL
- **ATL/CTL Target**: 1.0-1.1
- **TSB Target**: -5 to 5
- **Key Sessions**: Race-pace practice
- **Duration**: 2-4 weeks before race

### TAPER BLOCK
- **Goal**: Shed fatigue, maintain fitness
- **TSS Decrease**: 20-30% first week, 40-60% race week
- **ATL/CTL Target**: Decreasing
- **TSB Target**: 0 to +15 on race day
- **Key Sessions**: Short race-pace openers
- **Duration**: 7-14 days

## RECOVERY GUIDELINES

### Sleep Requirements
- **Optimal**: 8-9 hours
- **Minimum**: 7 hours
- **Quality markers**: Sleep score > 85%, low restlessness

### Nutrition Timing
- **During workout**: 60-90g carbs/hour (>90 min sessions)
- **Post-workout**: 20-40g protein + carbs within 30 min
- **Daily protein**: 1.6-2.2 g/kg body weight
- **Carbs on heavy days**: 6-10 g/kg body weight

### Active Recovery
- **Frequency**: 1-2 days per week
- **Intensity**: Zone 1 only (<60% FTP, <70% HR)
- **Duration**: 30-60 minutes
- **Purpose**: Blood flow, not training stress

## FUELING STRATEGIES

### Race-Day Fueling
- **Pre-race**: 2-3g carbs/kg, 3-4 hours before
- **During race**: 
  - Sprint/Olympic: 30-60g/hour
  - Half-Ironman: 60-90g/hour
  - Ironman: 90-120g/hour
- **Post-race**: 1.2g carbs/kg + 0.3g protein/kg

### Training Fueling
- **Low-intensity (<2 hours)**: Water + electrolytes
- **Moderate (2-3 hours)**: 30-60g carbs/hour
- **High-intensity (>3 hours)**: 60-90g carbs/hour

## TESTING & OVERRIDE RULES

### When to Override Calculations
1. **Recent illness**: Reduce TSS by 30-50%
2. **Travel/jetlag**: Reduce by 20-30%
3. **Life stress**: Reduce by 10-20%
4. **Injury risk**: Immediate reduction
5. **Pregnancy/postpartum**: Custom protocols

### Mandatory Rest Triggers
- TSB < -30 for 3+ consecutive days
- HRV < baseline - 20% for 2+ consecutive days
- Sleep < 6 hours for 3+ consecutive nights
- Orthopedic pain increasing
- Illness symptoms (fever, persistent cough)
