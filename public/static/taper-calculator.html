Taper Guidelines
Important Taper Principles
  • Maintain Intensity: Keep workout intensity high, but drastically reduce volume
  • Preserve Frequency: Continue regular training sessions, just shorter
  • Listen to Your Body: If you feel fatigued, reduce more. If restless, add short sessions
  • Final Week: Minimal training 4-5 days before race. Day before: 15-20 min easy + 3-5 x 20sec pickups
  • Nutrition: Increase carbs by 10-20% in final 2-3 days. Stay hydrated
  • Sleep: Prioritize 8+ hours. Go to bed earlier than usual
  • Trust the Taper: Feeling sluggish in week 1-2 is normal. You'll feel sharp race week
