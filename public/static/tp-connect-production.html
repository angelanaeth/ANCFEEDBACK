Connect TrainingPeaks

Use your existing TrainingPeaks coach account

Requirements
  • You must have an existing TrainingPeaks coach account
  • Your account should have athletes you want to manage
  • You'll authorize Echodevo to access your athlete data

1 Generate Authorization Link

Click the button below to generate your TrainingPeaks authorization link.

Your Authorization URL:

2 Authorize with TrainingPeaks

  1. Open the authorization URL in a new tab
  2. Log in with your TrainingPeaks coach account
  3. Review the permissions being requested
  4. Click "Allow" or "Authorize"
Expected Behavior: After authorizing, TrainingPeaks will try to redirect to http://127.0.0.1:5000/... which will show an error (this is normal since we're not running on localhost).

3 Complete Authorization

After clicking "Allow", copy the entire URL from your browser's address bar and paste it below.

The URL should start with http://127.0.0.1:5000/handle_trainingpeaks_authorization?code=...
